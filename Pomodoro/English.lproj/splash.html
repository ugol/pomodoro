Timer Help

Timer is a lightweight application for Personal Time Management.

Timer stays silently and unobtrusively on your status bar.
To start a Timer, just click on the timer icon and choose 'Start Timer' from the menu, or use the Keyboard Shortcut

Add a meaningful description, press Ok and timer starts. That's all.

You have all sort of notifications and integrations at your fingertips: just click on Preferences to customise Timer in every detail.

Visit the website for more detailed help on how to use Timer.
