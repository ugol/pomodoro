Pomodoro Help

Pomodoro is a lightweight application for Time Management.

Pomodoro stays silently and unobtrusively on your status bar.
To start a Pomodoro, just click on the timer icon and choose 'Start Pomodoro' from the menu, or use the Keyboard Shortcut

Add a meaningful description, press Ok and pomodoro starts. That's all.

You have all sort of notifications and integrations at your fingertips: just click on Preferences to customise Pomodoro in every detail.

Visit the documentation page for more detailed help on how to use Pomodoro.
